The Full Experience Menu

Every night is programmed.
Every experience drives revenue.

RASA is not a DJ bar. It is a full-spectrum cultural experience venue. Different experiences attract different crowds — and different revenue models.

01
🎵

Bollywood & Tollywood DJ Nights

The heartbeat. Friday Tollywood, Saturday Bollywood. Full DJ sets, cover charges, peak cocktail service. The highest revenue nights of the week — 70% of weekly income in 15 hours of operation.

Primary · Fri & Sat · Cover + Bar
02
🎤

Desi Karaoke Nights

Bollywood karaoke — Hindi, Telugu, Punjabi, Tamil. Private booth packages available for groups. One of the fastest-growing bar formats globally at 6%+ CAGR. Drives longer dwell time and higher per-head spend than DJ nights. The community loves to perform.

Mid-week · Group bookings · Private booths
03
🎸

Live Band Nights

Ghazal evenings, Bollywood jazz fusion, live fusion bands blending Indian classical with contemporary. Positions RASA as a cultural institution, not just a party venue. Attracts an older, higher-spending demographic on weeknights.

Thu & select nights · Premium cover
04
👑

Girls Night Out

Monthly dedicated girls night — DJ, special cocktail menu, priority reservations, themed décor. The desi girls night is massively underserved in NJ. Women are the primary decision-makers for group outings. Own this night and you own the group booking market.

Monthly · Group packages · High bar spend
05
🏏

IPL & Cricket Watch Parties

IPL season is 2 months of packed midweek nights. India vs Pakistan match = a sold-out venue before doors open. Live screening, themed cocktails, group viewing packages. Turns Tuesday into Saturday revenue. Completely untapped in Edison's nightlife space.

Seasonal · Midweek revenue booster
06
🕯️

Private Events & Sangeets

Sangeets, engagement parties, milestone birthdays, Diwali corporate events, baby showers. Full venue buyout at $5K–$25K per event. Recession-resilient — life events happen regardless of the economy. The most profitable revenue stream per hour of operation.

$5K–$25K per event · Venue buyout
07
🎭

Comedy & Open Mic Nights

Desi comedy is exploding. South Asian comedians are selling out venues nationwide. A monthly desi comedy night at RASA creates a completely new audience segment — families, professionals, people who don't typically go to bars but will come for a show.

Monthly · Ticket + bar · New audience
08
🪔

Cultural Festival Nights

Navratri. Diwali. Holi. Lohri. Onam. Eid. Each festival night targets a specific sub-community and builds breadth of loyalty. The Diwali grand opening event alone could be the most talked-about night in Edison in a decade.

Annual events · Premium pricing · Sold out
09
🌙

Retro & Theme Nights

90s Bollywood only. 2000s special. Ar Rahman tribute night. Shah Rukh Khan vs Salman Khan — you choose a side, the DJ plays your team. Themed nights create social media buzz before the event even happens. The concept is the marketing.

Monthly · Cover charge · High social reach
