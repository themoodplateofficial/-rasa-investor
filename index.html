01 · The Concept

Not a restaurant.
Not a nightclub.
The modern desi adda.

RASA brings the energy of India's social culture into a premium, design-led nightlife experience — where music, drinks, and community come together effortlessly.

Every Indian-American in central New Jersey knows the feeling — you want a great night out, desi music, good drinks, a space that feels like yours. So you drive 45 minutes to Manhattan. Then you drive 45 minutes back.

RASA ends that journey. We are building Edison's first permanent, dedicated desi lounge — a space that is casual enough for any Thursday, electric enough for every Saturday, and rooted deeply in Indian culture and community.

The name RASA comes from Sanskrit. In classical Indian art, the nine rasas are the nine fundamental human emotions. Our concept is built on that philosophy — every night at RASA is designed to make you feel something specific.

Craft Indian Bar

Not a cocktail menu. A cultural passport. Every drink at RASA is named after an icon from across the Indian diaspora — Telugu blockbusters, Bollywood dialogues, Punjabi anthems, ABCD experiences. The first pan-Indian cocktail program in the United States. Not just Bollywood. All of India.

RRR Naatu Naatu Baahubali Pushpa Desi at Heart

When someone orders a "Thaggede le" shot, the whole table erupts. That moment of recognition — that connection — is what makes RASA unreplicable.

2

Bolly / Tolly Nights

Live DJ programming Thursday through Sunday. Dedicated Tollywood Fridays for the Telugu-speaking community — the largest underserved South Asian sub-group in central NJ. Bollywood Saturdays as the main event.

3

Bar Snacks & Hookah

Elevated Indian bar snacks inspired by nostalgic favorites — designed to pair effortlessly with cocktails and recreate the energy of classic nights out. Simple, familiar, and craveable — built to keep guests engaged and coming back. A premium hookah offering extends the experience and adds to the social atmosphere.

4

Instagram-Driven Design

A signature peacock-inspired installation, expressed through abstract geometry and layered lighting, anchors the DJ and bar — defining the visual identity of RASA. The space is modern and minimal, designed to drive organic sharing and repeat engagement.

02 · Market Opportunity

They're already in Edison.
Give them a reason to stay.

Edison is already one of the most visited South Asian destinations in America. With a population of 110,002, a median household income of $125,145, and over 50% Asian residents — the highest concentration of any municipality in New Jersey — it draws visitors from across the tri-state area, not just to live here, but to experience it.

Oak Tree Road is the anchor. Confirmed by NJ PBS as an internationally recognized South Asian destination, it is the largest and most diverse South Asian cultural hub in the United States — drawing shoppers, diners, and families from New Jersey, New York, and beyond every single weekend. Middlesex County, home to Edison, has the highest concentration of Asian Indians of any county in the United States, with a median household income of $111,549.

34.9% of Edison's residents identify as Indian American — the highest percentage of any municipality in the country. Add the broader South Asian community — Gujaratis, Punjabis, Telugus, Sri Lankans — and the audience is even larger.

The visitors are already here. The spending is already happening. What disappears at 9pm is the opportunity — because there is no premium nightlife venue built for this audience anywhere in central New Jersey.

RASA doesn't need to build its customer base from scratch. We position ourselves as the natural next stop for every person who spends their Saturday on Oak Tree Road. Shop in the afternoon. Dine at sunset. Come to RASA when the night begins.

"We are not asking people to travel somewhere new. We are giving them a reason to stay."

Bollywood events in Edison today exist only as pop-ups at non-Indian venues — rented spaces with no permanent identity. There is no dedicated, designed, permanently open premium desi lounge in all of central NJ. RASA is the night Edison has been missing.

Designed for margin, dwell time, and repeat behavior.

The core revenue driver, contributing the majority of sales. Premium cocktails operate at ~65–75% gross margin, with a menu designed around high-frequency ordering and strong pricing power.
